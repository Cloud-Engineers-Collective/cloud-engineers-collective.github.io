About Us

The Cloud Engineers Collective is a not for profit organisation, governed by members for it's members. Members are vetted to ensure that they are reputuble and professional cloud engineers who have a proven track history of delivering Cloud Native solutions to their clients.

We provide a network of support, sharing our skills, knowledge and experiences for the benefit of our members and the wider tech community.

We have a set of principle that all members have agreed to adhear to, you can think of them in a similar vain to the agile manefesto.
