Cloud Engineers Collective

About Us

The CEC is a not for profit organisation, governed by members for it's members.

Members are vetted to ensure that they are reputuble and professional cloud engineers with a proven track history of delivering Cloud Native solutions to their clients.

What we do

Our community provide a network of support, sharing our skills, knowledge and experiences for the benefit of our members and other audiences.

How we work

We are governed by a set of principles, which all members have agreed to adhear to. You can think of them in a similar vain to the agile manefesto and code of conduct.
